Our Story

2010 - Foundation

MediCare Clinic was founded by Dr. James Wilson with a vision to create a patient-centered healthcare facility that prioritizes compassionate care and medical excellence.

2014 - Expansion

Expanded our facility to include a state-of-the-art diagnostic center and added specialist services in cardiology, orthopedics, and pediatrics.

2018 - Accreditation

Achieved Joint Commission accreditation and implemented electronic health records system for improved patient care coordination.

2023 - Innovation

Launched telemedicine services and community health programs, serving over 50,000 patients with comprehensive healthcare solutions.
